\begin{enpars}
O Joseph, the honor of those in heaven,
And our hope of life and the support of the world,
Kindly receive the praises
That we sing joyfully sing unto thee.

The Creator of the world appointed thee
the spouse of the most pure Virgin, and he
willed that thou be called the father of the
Word; and he made thee a minister of
salvation.

Rejoicing thou didst behold the Redeemer
lying in the stable, him whose advent
the choir of prophets had foretold; and thou
didst humbly adore the new-born God.

God, the King of
kings, and the ruler of the world, at whose
nod the hosts of hell tremble, whom the
heavens prostrate serve, makes himself
subject to thee.

Never-ending praise be to the most
high Trinity who hath bestowed upon
thee heavenly honors, and may the same
grant us, through thy merits, the joys of a blessed life. Amen.
\end{enpars}
\end{multicols}
