\begin{enpars} \noindent Alleluia, Alleluia, Alleluia.
\end{enpars}

\begin{multicols}{2}
\raggedcolumns
\begin{enpars}
O sons and daughters, the celestial King, the glorious King,
from death arose today.  Alleluia.

 Early on the first day of the week, the disciples approached the entrance of the tomb.  Alleluia.
     
Mary Magdalene, and Mary of James, and Salome, came to anoint the body. Alleluia.

A white-robed Angel sitting there, foretold unto the women: The Lord is in
Galilee. Alleluia.

The Apostle John quickly outran Peter, and came first to the tomb. Alleluia. 

As the disciples stood together, in their midst stood Christ, and said: Peace be
to all of you. Alleluia.

When word reached the Twin that Jesus had risen, he persisted in his doubt. Alleluia.

Behold, Thomas, see my side, see my feet, see my hands; be unbelieving no more. Alleluia.

When Thomas beheld Christ’s side, his feet, his hands, he proclaimed: Thou
art my God. Alleluia.

Blessed are they who have not seen, yet firmly believed; they shall have life
eternal. Alleluia.

On this most holy feast, let there be praise and jubilation, and let us bless the
Lord. Alleluia.

For these things, let us most humbly, devoutly and dutifully, render thanks to
God. Alleluia.
\end{enpars}\end{multicols}
