O God, who though unseen, upholdest all things and yet for the salvation of mankind dost visibly show signs of thy power: give glory to this temple by the might of thine indwelling, and grant that all who in their deep distress shall come and call upon thee here may receive thy divine comfort. Through Jesus Christ, thy Son our Lord, who liveth and reigneth with thee, in the unity of the Holy Ghost, God, world without end.
