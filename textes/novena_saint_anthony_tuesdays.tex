{\centering\Large{\MakeUppercase{\capspace{tuesday novena to saint anthony.}}}\par}

\begin{enpars}

\rubrique{Said kneeling.}

\lettrine{O}{} glorious Saint Anthony, * safe refuge of the afflicted and distressed, * who by miraculous revelation * hast directed all those who seek aid to come to thine altar * with the promise * that whoever visits it for nine consecutive Tuesdays * and there piously invokes thee, * will feel the power of thine intercession. * I, a poor sinner, * encouraged by this promise, * come to thee, o powerful Saint, * and with a firm hope I implore thine aid, * thy protection, * thy counsel, * and thy blessing.

\lettrine{O}{b}tain for me, I beseech thee, * my request in this necessity (think of your request). * But if it should be opposed to the will of God, * the welfare of my soul, obtain for me such other graces * as shall be conducive to my salvation. * Through Christ our Lord. Amen.

\vv Our Father…

\rr Give us this day…

\vv Hail Mary…

\rr Holy Mary…

\vv Glory be…

\rr As it was…world without end. Amen.

\vv Pray for us, o blessed Anthony.

\rr That we may be made worthy of the promises of Christ.

\rubrique{Priest:}

Almighty and eternal God, who didst glorify thy faithful confessor Anthony with the perpetual gift of working miracles: graciously grant, that what we confidently seek through his merits, we may surely receive by his intercession. Through Christ Our Lord.

\rr Amen.

\rubrique{The priest blesses the faithful with the relic. Then:
}
\rubrique{Priest:}

If thou dost ask for miracles,
death, error, all calamities,
the leprosy and demons fly,
and health succeeds infirmities.

\rr The sea obeys, and fetters break, and lifeless limbs thou dost restore; whilst treasures lost are found again when young or old thine aid implore.

\rubrique{Priest:}

All dangers vanish at thy prayer,
and direst need doth quickly flee,
let those who know thy power proclaim, 
let Paduans say, these are of thee.

\rr The sea obeys, etc.

To Father, Son may glory be,
and Holy Ghost eternally.

\rr The sea obeys.
\end{enpars}
