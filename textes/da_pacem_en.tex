\begin{enpars}\noindent 
Grant peace, Lord, in our time;
for there is none else
who would fight for us
if not thou, our God.
\end{enpars}

{\noindent \vv Let peace be in thy strength.

\noindent \rr And abundance in thy towers.

\noindent Let us pray. O God, from whom all holy desires, all good counsels, and all just works do proceed; give unto thy servants that peace which the world cannot give; so that, with our hearts set to obey thy commandments, and freed from the fear of the enemy, we may pass our lives in peace under thy protection. Through Christ our Lord. Amen.}
