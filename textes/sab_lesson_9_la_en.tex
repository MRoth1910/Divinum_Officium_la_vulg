Dicens: Hic sanguis testaménti, quod mandávit ad vos Deus. 
Etiam tabernáculum, et ó\-mnia vasa ministérii sánguine simíliter aspérsit: 
Et ó\-mnia pene in sánguine secúndum legem mundántur: et sine sánguinis effusióne non fit remíssio.}{english}{\noindent For when every commandment of the law had been read by Moses to all the people, he took the blood of calves and goats, with water, and scarlet wool and hyssop, and sprinkled both the book itself and all the people,
Saying: This is the blood of the testament, which God hath enjoined unto you.
The tabernacle also and all the vessels of the ministry, in like manner, he sprinkled with blood.
And almost all things, according to the law, are cleansed with blood: and without shedding of blood there is no remission.}
