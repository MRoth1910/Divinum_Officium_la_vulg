\begin{enpars}
 \item Blessed be the Lord \cc{} God of Israel;~* because he hath visited and wrought the redemption of his people:
 
 \item And hath raised up an horn of salvation to us,~* in the house of David his servant:
 
\item As he spoke by the mouth of his holy Prophets,~* who are from the beginning:
 
 \item Salvation from our enemies,~* and from the hand of all that hate us:
 
 \item To perform mercy to our fathers,~* and to remember his holy testament.
 
\item The oath, which he swore to Abraham our father,~* that he would grant to us.
 
 \item That being delivered from the hand of our enemies,~* we may serve him without fear.
 
\item In holiness and justice before him,~* all our days.
 
\item And thou, child, shalt be called the prophet of the Highest:~* for thou shalt go before the face of the Lord to prepare his ways:
 
\item To give knowledge of salvation to his people,~* unto the remission of their sins:
 
\item Through the bowels of the mercy of our God,~* in which the Orient from on high hath visited us:
 
 \item To enlighten them that sit in darkness, and in the shadow of death:~* to direct our feet into the way of peace.
 \end{enpars}
