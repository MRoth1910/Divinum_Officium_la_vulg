\begin{enpars}
\lettrine{O} Lord, open thou my mouth that I may bless thy Holy Name. Cleanse my heart from all vain, evil, and wandering thoughts; enlighten my understanding kindle my affections, that I may pray to, and praise thee with attention and devotion; and may worthily be heard in the presence of thy Divine Majesty. Through Christ our Lord. Amen.

Lord, in union with that Divine Intention wherewith thou didst thyself praise God, whilst thou wast on earth, I offer these Hours unto thee.
\end{enpars}
