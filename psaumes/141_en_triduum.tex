\begin{enpars}
\item I cried to the Lord with my voice:~* with my voice I made supplication to the Lord.
\item In his sight I pour out my prayer,~* and before him I declare my trouble:
\item When my spirit failed me,~* then thou knewest my paths.
\item In this way wherein I walked,~* they have hidden a snare for me.
\item I looked on my right hand, and beheld,~* and there was no one that would know me.
\item Flight hath failed me:~* and there is no one that hath regard to my soul.
\item I cried to thee, o Lord:~* I said: Thou art my hope, my portion in the land of the living.
\item Attend to my supplication:~* for I am brought very low.
\item Deliver me from my persecutors;~* for they are stronger than I.
\item Bring my soul out of prison, that I may praise thy name:~* the just wait for me, until thou reward me.
\end{enpars}
