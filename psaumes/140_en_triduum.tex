\begin{enpars}
\item I have cried to thee, o Lord, hear me:~* hearken to my voice, when I cry to thee.
\item Let my prayer be directed as incense in thy sight;~* the lifting up of my hands, as evening sacrifice.
\item Set a watch, o Lord, before my mouth:~* and a door round about my lips.
\item Incline not my heart to evil words;~* to make excuses in sins.
\item With men that work iniquity:~* and I will not communicate with the choicest of them.
\item The just man shall correct me in mercy, and shall reprove me:~* but let not the oil of the sinner fatten my head.
\item For my prayer also shall still be against the things with which they are well pleased:~* their judges falling upon the rock have been swallowed up.
\item They shall hear my words, for they have prevailed:~* as when the thickness of the earth is broken up upon the ground.
\item Our bones are scattered by the side of hell.~* But to thee, o Lord, Lord, are my eyes: in thee have I put my trust, take not away my soul.
\item Keep me from the snare, which they have laid for me,~* and from the stumbling blocks of them that work iniquity.
\item The wicked shall fall in his net:~* I am alone until I pass.
\end{enpars}
