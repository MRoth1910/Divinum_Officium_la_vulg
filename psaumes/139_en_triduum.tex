\begin{enpars}
\item Deliver me, O Lord, from the evil man:~* rescue me from the unjust man.
\item Who have devised iniquities in their hearts:~* all the day long they designed battles.
\item They have sharpened their tongues like a serpent:~* the venom of asps is under their lips.
\item Keep me, O Lord, from the hand of the wicked:~* and from unjust men deliver me.
\item Who have proposed to supplant my steps:~* the proud have hidden a net for me.
\item And they have stretched out cords for a snare:~* they have laid for me a stumbling block by the wayside.
\item I said to the Lord: Thou art my God:~* hear, O Lord, the voice of my supplication.
\item O Lord, Lord, the strength of my salvation:~* thou hast overshadowed my head in the day of battle.
\item Give me not up, O Lord, from my desire to the wicked:~* they have plotted against me; do not thou forsake me, lest they should triumph.
\item The head of them compassing me about:~* the labour of their lips shall overwhelm them.
\item Burning coals shall fall upon them; thou wilt cast them down into the fire:~* in miseries they shall not be able to stand.
\item A man full of tongue shall not be established in the earth:~* evil shall catch the unjust man unto destruction.
\item I know that the Lord will do justice to the needy,~* and will revenge the poor.
\item But as for the just, they shall give glory to thy name:~* and the upright shall dwell with thy countenance.
\end{enpars}
