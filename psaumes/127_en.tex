\item 1. Blessed are all they that fear the Lord:~*that walk in his ways.
\item 2. For thou shalt eat the labours of thy hands:~*blessed art thou, and it shall be well with thee.
\item 3. Thy wife as a fruitful vine,~*on the sides of thy house.
\item 4. Thy children as olive plants,~*round about thy table.
\item 5. Behold, thus shall the man be blessed~*that feareth the Lord.
\item 6. May the Lord bless thee out of Sion:~*and mayst thou see the good things of Jerusalem all the days of thy life.
\item 7. And mayst thou see thy children’s children,~*peace upon Israel.
\item 8. Glory be to the Father, and to the Son,~*and to the Holy Ghost. 
\item 9. As it was in the beginning, is now,~*and ever shall be, world without end. Amen.
